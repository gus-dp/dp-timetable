What is the IB Diploma Programme? 🎓

The International Baccalaureate® (IB) Diploma Programme (DP) is a rigorous academic program for students aged 16 to 19, recognized by universities around the world! 🌍

Through the DP, students develop:

  • 📘 Excellent breadth and depth of knowledge
  • 🤔 Critical thinking and research skills
  • 🗣️ Language proficiency and global awareness
  • 🏆 Strong leadership and ethical commitment
  • 🎯 A well-rounded academic experience

For more details, visit the official IB website: What is the DP? 🌐
